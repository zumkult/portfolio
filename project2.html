ILES FORMULA is a luxury professional hair care brand founded by stylist Wendy Iles.

As part of our collaboration, I developed print and digital materials for the brand: catalog design, postcards, and promotional materials, as well as online banners and visuals for mailings and e-commerce platforms. My work included concept and visual storytelling, layout and typography, image retouching, pre-press preparation, print coordination, and approval management to ensure a consistent premium brand image across all communication channels.
