SIMPLIE HAIR is a brand specializing in hair extensions and professional training for stylists.

As part of our collaboration, I was responsible for all of the brand's visual and print content: developing multi-page workbooks and B2B brochures, designing packaging, organizing and producing product and model photo shoots, and preparing materials for print and digital use. My work included concept development, layout, visual storytelling design, pre-press preparation, production control, and post-production of images for the website, e-commerce, and social media.
